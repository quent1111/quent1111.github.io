Important

Any surgical intervention must be preceded by a consultation by appointment, for which you must absolutely bring:

  • The referral letter from your doctor, dentist or orthodontist indicating the reason for the consultation
  • Your radiographs, especially a panoramic dental x-ray (an image of ALL the teeth) for oral problems
  • The prescription of your usual daily treatments if you are taking any
  • The appropriate certificate if you are a beneficiary of CMU, CSS, ACS or AME
  • In case of unforeseen circumstances, please inform the medical office of your absence at least 48 hours in advance; any unjustified absence may permanently prohibit any new appointment.
